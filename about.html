Our Mission

Our goal is to improve the quality of your life, prevent unwanted hospital visits,

provide education on your condition and medications so you and your family

can be active participants in your care and provide coordination between

healthcare providers in times of emergencies.

Monitoring - anytime, anywhere

Our vital signs self-monitoring system uses simple technology to proactively

monitor patients remotely. Patients who need continuous monitoring can use

the device to measure their blood pressure, temperature, heart rate and

oxygen intake continuously without having to travel to clinic or clinicians

having to visit them to collect simple readings.

About Us

Heart Watch is India’s largest predictive healthcare analytics firm, catering to the needs

of cardiac patients. We realized that a significant portion of Indians suffering from heart

diseases are uninsured and are far less likely to receive appropriate and timely medical

care. They also often suffer worse medical outcomes, including higher mortality rates.

Heart Watch leverages its deep medical and technological expertise, to provide constant

Monitoring and Support Services to people with cardiac issues.

Over time, we have evolved and developed into a tech-driven cardiac care organisation

backed by a highly experienced medical and technological team, allowing us to develop

systems which significantly enhance the detection of rare cardiac disorders.
